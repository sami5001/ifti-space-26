## Project Overview

Completed a Master of Science in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Stuttgart, one of Germany's leading technical universities for aerospace research. The program included advanced coursework and research in high-speed aerodynamics.

### Specializations

- **Mathematical and Physical Modeling** - Advanced techniques for modeling complex aerospace systems
- **Jet Propulsion** - Theory and design of aircraft and rocket propulsion systems
- **High-Speed Aerodynamics** - Fundamentals of supersonic and hypersonic flow

### Research Experience

#### Visiting Research Scholar (2015-2016)
**University of Arizona, CFD Laboratory**

Supported by the prestigious Erich-Becker Scholarship, conducted research at the University of Arizona's Computational Fluid Dynamics Laboratory. This international collaboration provided valuable experience in advanced simulation techniques for high-speed flows.

### Key Outcomes

- Strong foundation in theoretical and computational aerodynamics
- International research experience through Arizona collaboration
- Preparation for doctoral research in hypersonics at Oxford
- Network with leading researchers in the field
