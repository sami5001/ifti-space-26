## Project Overview

Completed a Bachelor of Science in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Stuttgart, with thesis work conducted at Airbus Deutschland GmbH in Hamburg. This industry placement provided hands-on experience with one of the world's most advanced commercial aircraft programs.

### Thesis Work

The bachelor's thesis was completed within the A380 Chief Engineering Department, working directly with the engineering team responsible for the world's largest passenger aircraft. The research made tangible contributions to the aircraft program.

### Industrial Impact

A key achievement of this work was that **the research results were subsequently implemented on the A380 final assembly line**. This demonstrates the practical relevance and quality of the research, as Airbus engineering standards are among the most stringent in the industry.

### Skills Developed

- Aircraft structural analysis and design
- Engineering documentation and standards
- Collaboration with multidisciplinary teams
- Understanding of aircraft certification requirements
- Experience with industry-standard tools and processes

### Significance

This industrial experience:
- Provided real-world engineering perspective
- Demonstrated ability to deliver research with practical impact
- Established foundation for academic research career
