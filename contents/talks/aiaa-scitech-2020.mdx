## Talk Abstract

Technical presentation on computational investigation of coolant gas injection effects on hypersonic boundary layer characteristics. The talk covered high-fidelity numerical simulations and their implications for thermal protection system design.

## Key Points

1. Boundary layer physics at hypersonic speeds
2. Numerical simulation methodology
3. Coolant injection strategies
4. Results on boundary layer modification
5. Design recommendations for thermal protection systems
