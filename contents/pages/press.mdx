## Research Highlights

### AIAA SPACE 2018 Presentation

Featured presentation at the AIAA SPACE Forum 2018 in Orlando, Florida, showcasing breakthrough research on transpiration cooling technology for hypersonic vehicles. The research demonstrated temperature threshold capabilities up to 3500 K.

### Oxford Hypersonics Group

Contributing researcher to the Oxford Hypersonics Group, one of the world's leading research groups in high-speed aerothermodynamics and thermal protection systems.

## Outreach Features

### From Slums to Rocket Science

A STEM education initiative led in Bangladesh, introducing aerospace engineering concepts to underprivileged children. This project has been recognized for its impact on inspiring young minds to pursue careers in science and technology.
